invoice email setup

Plain HTML cannot send invoice emails directly from Gmail by itself. To make automatic order invoices work, connect simplyblissf@gmail.com to EmailJS, then paste your EmailJS details below. After that, every successful order will try to send an invoice email to the customer automatically.

  1. Create an EmailJS account and connect Gmail as the sending service.
  2. Use simplyblissf@gmail.com as the connected sender mailbox in EmailJS.
  3. Create an email template for invoices.
  4. Paste your public key, service ID and template ID below, then click save.
saved ✓
